About
Whiteboard is technology designed by healthcare professionals, for healthcare professionals.

The Whiteboard application found its genesis in medical professionals frustrated with inefficiencies in their industry.

This team of doctors and healthcare experts teamed up with business leaders and tech experts who brought experience with digital innovation to the table. Together, the Whiteboard founders developed an encrypted application that would save doctors time and improve patient outcomes, without compromising sensitive information.

Whiteboard is founded on two principles:
Facilitate greater and effective collaborative care of patients and their complex care needs.
Increase the flow, accuracy and timing of communication between healthcare professionals in realtime.
