What is Whiteboard?
It’s secure, real-time healthcare communication and collaboration. Whiteboard is a software application developed by healthcare professionals, for healthcare professionals. It’s a secure, encrypted portal that allows your patients and colleagues real-time access to share and collaborate on medical records.
Share it

Create and share specific notes, medications, documents or even entire health records with automatic updates in real-time

Talk about it

Instant Messaging functionality built specifically for healthcare professionals takes the hassle out of quick queries with a transparent audit trail.

Integrate it

The Whiteboard Sidebar integrates seamlessly with your existing medical software for safe extraction of clinical data items.

Remote it

Whiteboard works on any device, in any location with an internet connection. Collaborate with colleagues no matter where you are.
